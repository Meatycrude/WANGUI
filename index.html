About Me

My Journey

As a third-year Counseling Psychology student at Kisii University, I am deeply passionate about mental health advocacy and providing support to individuals navigating life's challenges.

My academic journey has equipped me with theoretical knowledge and practical skills in psychological assessment, therapeutic interventions, and mental health promotion.
